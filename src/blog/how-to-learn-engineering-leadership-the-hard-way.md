In every builder's journey, whether you're going it solo or with a small, tight knit founding team, there comes an inflection point where there are far more plates than there are hands to spin them. No matter how hard you or your team try, there's just no more stretch left to cover everything.

And so the inevitable conversation comes up: **Let's hire someone**. No one has any time to take any courses on leadership or management of course, we're all too busy building. Chances are very likely no one really has any real prior experience either. If you or anyone on your team does, count yourself very lucky.
